About Us
Kitchen chimney being one of the most effective home appliance, nowadays, need to be serviced and maintained accordingly. We at Jai Ganesh Enterprises have a wide range of services to offer for your convenience. We are located at Arumbakkam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Our expert servicemen are available round the clock to provide repairing services of chimneys and hobs to render its longevity. Our expertise also lies in rendering installation of the kitchen chimneys of varied brands. Count on us to provide proficient cleaning services of your kitchen chimneys.
